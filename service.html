OUR SERVICES

SMART HOMES

this is the ability to control almost everything in your home from a device. this beats the traditional method of manually controlling things.

DOOR AUTOMATION

Our automated door systems are one of the most modernized systems that beats the traditional door locks we have around, it is fully configured and highly secure. This type of automation enables you to access your home doors with either an access card, your finger print, or your mobile devices. This system is very unique and can stand the taste of time. With this you can solve the problem of misplacing keys and also the issue of forgetting to lock up, once you pass the door locks automatically.

AUTOMATED LIGHTING CONTROL

Our automated lighting control systems are one of the best, they are very convenient and come with different features, one of which is the ability to be controlled by mobile devices, you can easily use their mobile phones to turn on/off the light without having to move and look for the switch, you can also walk into the bathroom and the light comes on by itself, and when you walk out it turns off, that is another feature that works with motion sensors. All these are classy and is a necessity for every home.

AUTOMATED CURTAIN CONTROL

Most times we are too lazy to get up and do anything. With this feature they you get to open and close the curtain or blinds by yourself at your convenience. All these features don’t just make things easier but it makes your home smart and modern plus it gives you a comfortable standard of living.

AUTOMATED FURNITURE

With this system your furniture’s will be automated like your bed, stools, chairs, drawers etc. they can all be controlled by your mobile devices you can open them, close them, adjust them, for your bed you can cover it up and adjust it. All these gives your home some extra beauty and comfort.

ENTERTAINMENT DEVICES

includes tvs, wireless speakers, film projectors, all of these devices can be turned on from any part of the room using your mobile device and also some extra control features like volume regulation, change of channel and many other extra settings.

HIDDEN ACCESSORIES

We automate some accessories in your home so they can be hidden and they only show up when you want them to, with the use of your mobile device. Accessories like your tv can be hidden in the wall or the ceiling depending on your preferred location, speakers, sound systems etc.

SMART HOTEL SYSTEM

this is the ability for guests to have control over things in their hotel room with the use of their mobile devices. Also a door automation system where cards are used to gain access to the rooms, motion sensors and other operational activities

GENERAL ACCESS CONTROL

This can be done for both hotels, offices, factories etc. With this system you get to control access (entry and exist)

CCTV, INTERCOM & INTERNET NETWORKING

We set up the best surveillance systems and we get to link them to your devices both online and offline. We also set up intercom systems for internal and external communications. We provide internet connection at a very high-speed connection and amazing network uptime.

LED DISPLAY BOARDS

We make super amazing led display boards that showcases small and big businesses with all kinds of modules (2, 3, 4, etc).

GATE AUTOMATION

This system that is set up for you to be in-charge of your own security, you do not need to get a gate man for yourself, you can be your own gate man from anywhere, open your gate with the use of your device which will be configured to your automated gate. This saves you time and money most importantly and also it gives you total control of who you let in or out of your house.

GENERAL EMBEDED SYSTEMS

We help procure and install all kinds of embedded systems like Arduino, relay etc.

ELECTRICAL WORKS AND ELECTRONICS

We proffer full installation of electrical works and also electronics for either your home, office, hotels, factories etc.
