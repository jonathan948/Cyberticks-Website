ABOUT US

Cyberticks Integrated Solutions is aimed at improving the lives of individuals and businesses by developing solutions to everyday problems. We believe when creativity, technology and dedication combine through strategic planning, incredible results are achievable. Part of the services we offer are device manufacturing, home automation, process automation, access automation. We pride ourselves in exceptional delivery and state of the art finishing. At Cyberticks Integrated Solutions we are a group of creatives and tech savvy individuals dedicated to developing digital solutions to drive businesses to maximum scale. At Cyberticks our team of software/ Hardware developers, programmers and business developers are all driven by a common goal to install excellence in businesses and enterprises.
